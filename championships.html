Major Jiu Jitsu Championships

The IBJJF World Championship, also known as "Mundials," is the pinnacle of competitive BJJ. Held annually, it draws elite practitioners from across the globe to test their skills in gi-based competition.

Other major events include:

  • ADCC Submission Fighting World Championship – The most prestigious no-gi tournament, blending BJJ with wrestling and judo.
  • Pan Jiu-Jitsu Championship – One of the largest tournaments in the Americas, attracting top-level talent.
  • UAEJJF Abu Dhabi World Pro – A high-profile competition with significant cash prizes and international competitors.
