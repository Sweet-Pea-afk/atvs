Jiu Jitsu World Records

Gabi Garcia holds multiple titles in BJJ, including ADCC and IBJJF championships. She has been recognized by the Guinness Book of World Records for her dominance in women's heavyweight divisions.

Other notable records include:

  • Marcus "Buchecha" Almeida – Holds the most IBJJF World Championship gold medals of all time.
  • Roger Gracie – Known for submitting all 10 of his opponents at the 2005 Mundials, a historic clean sweep.
  • Youngest BJJ Black Belt – Mica Galvão earned his black belt at just 17 years old, setting a record for the modern era.
