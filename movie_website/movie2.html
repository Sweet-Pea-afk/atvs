How to Train Your Dragon 2

The Movie Overview 

    "How to Train Your Dragon 2" is a 2014 computer-animated adventure film produced by DreamWorks Animation.
    The movie is a sequel to the 2010 film "How to Train Your Dragon" and continues the story of Hiccup 
    Horrendous Haddock III and his dragon, Toothless. The movie takes place five years after the events of 
    the first film and follows Hiccup as he discovers a hidden world of dragons and meets a new dragon, the 
    majestic and powerful Dragonite.

    From the award-nominated film How to Train Your Dragon comes the next chapter in the epic trilogy. 
    When Hiccup and Toothless discover a secret ice cave filled with hundreds of wild dragons and a mysterious
    Dragon Rider, the two friends find themselves at the center of an epic battle to save the future of men and
    dragons!


Awards and Accolades 

        "How to Train Your Dragon 2" was a critical and commercial success, grossing over $618 million worldwide. 
        The movie received widespread acclaim for its storytelling, animation, and voice acting. It won several 
        awards, including the Annie Award for Best Animated Feature, the BAFTA Award for Best Animated Film, and 
        the Critics' Choice Movie Award for Best Animated Feature. The movie was also nominated for several other 
        awards, including the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature. The success of the movie cemented the "How 
        to Train Your Dragon" franchise as one of the most beloved and successful animated franchises of all time.
