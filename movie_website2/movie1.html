How to Train Your Dragon
The Movie Overview
"How to Train Your Dragon" is a 2010 computer-animated adventure film produced by DreamWorks Animation. The movie is set in the fictional Viking village of Berk, where dragons and humans have been at war for centuries. The story follows Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III, a scrawny and awkward Viking teenager who lives with his father, Stoick the Vast, the village chieftain. Hiccup's life takes a dramatic turn when he encounters a Night Fury dragon, a rare and elusive species. This film rolls fire-breathing action, epic adventure and big laughs into a captivating, fun and original story. Hiccup is a young Viking who defies tradition and befriends one of his deadliest foes - a ferocious dragon he calls Toothless. Together, the unlikely heroes fight against the odds to save both their worlds in this wonderful, feel-good hit.


Awards and Accolades
"How to Train Your Dragon" was a critical and commercial success, grossing over $494 million worldwide. The movie received widespread acclaim for its storytelling, animation, and voice acting. It was nominated for several awards, including the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature, and won several others, including the BAFTA Award for Best Animated Film.
