How to Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World
The Movie Overview
"How to Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World" is a 2019 computer-animated adventure film produced by DreamWorks Animation. The movie is a sequel to the 2014 film "How to Train Your Dragon 2" and concludes the story of Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III and his dragon, Toothless. The movie takes place after the events of the second film and follows Hiccup as he tries to protect the dragons and the humans from a new threat, a group of dragon hunters led by Grimmel, a ruthless and cunning dragon hunter. From DreamWorks Animation comes a surprising tale about growing up, finding the courage to face the unknown... and how nothing can ever train you to let go. What began as an unlikely friendship between an adolescent Viking and a fearsome Night Fury dragon has become an epic adventure spanning their lives.


Awards and Accolades
"How to Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World" was a critical and commercial success, grossing over $521 million worldwide. The movie received widespread acclaim for its storytelling, animation, and voice acting. It won several awards, including the Annie Award for Best Animated Feature, the Critics' Choice Movie Award for Best Animated Feature, and the Visual Effects Society Award for Outstanding Animated Character in an Animated Feature Motion Picture. The movie was also nominated for several other awards, including the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature. The movie's success marked the end of the "How to Train Your Dragon" trilogy, but the franchise continues to live on through various spin-offs and merchandise.
